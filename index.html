CaliBeach Finder Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for CaliBeach Finder Effective Date: 07/19/2025 CaliBeach Finder (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (“CaliBeach Finder” or the “App”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App. 1. Information We Collect We do not collect personally identifiable information from users. The only information we access is: Location Data: The App requests access to your device’s location solely to show your current position on the map and help you find nearby California beaches. 2. How We Use Your Information Your location data is used only in real-time and only within the App to: Display your current position on the map Help you discover nearby beaches We do not collect, store, or transmit your location or any other personal information to our servers or third parties. 3. Data Sharing We do not share your information with third parties. Because we do not collect or retain personal data, there is nothing to share or sell. 4. Data Retention We do not retain any user data. Your location is processed only on your device and only while the app is open and in use. 5. Children’s Privacy CaliBeach Finder does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is safe for users of all ages. 6. Your Consent By using CaliBeach Finder, you consent to the use of location data as described in this policy. 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
